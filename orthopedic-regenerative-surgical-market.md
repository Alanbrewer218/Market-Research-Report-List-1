<p><p>The orthopedic regenerative surgical market encompasses various treatment types aimed at enhancing healing and restoring function. Allografts involve transplanted bone or tissue from donors, promoting repair. Cell-based treatments utilize stem cells or progenitor cells to stimulate regeneration. Viscosupplements introduce hyaluronic acid to lubricate joints, alleviating pain and improving mobility. Synthetics are artificially created materials that mimic natural tissues, providing structural support and facilitating healing. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to diverse orthopedic conditions and patient needs.</p></p>
