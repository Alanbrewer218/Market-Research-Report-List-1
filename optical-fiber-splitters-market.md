<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Splitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the optical fiber splitter market, Type I and Type II splitters serve distinct purposes. Type I splitters, typically based on fused biconical taper technology, are designed for passive applications, delivering consistent signal distribution with minimal loss. In contrast, Type II splitters incorporate advanced technologies like planar lightwave circuits, enabling more intricate signal management and greater scalability. While Type I is favored for straightforward networking needs, Type II is often utilized in more complex systems, catering to diverse telecommunications and data center requirements.</p></p>
