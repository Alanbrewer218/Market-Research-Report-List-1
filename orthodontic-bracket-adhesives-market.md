<p><strong>The Orthodontic Bracket Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light-Curable</li><li>Chemical-Curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthodontic bracket adhesives market consists of two primary types: light-curable and chemical-curable adhesives. Light-curable adhesives utilize a specialized light source to initiate the curing process, ensuring quick setting times and strong bonds. They are popular for their ease of use and aesthetic benefits. In contrast, chemical-curable adhesives harden through a chemical reaction without the need for light, offering reliable performance and extended working times. Both types are essential for effectively bonding brackets to teeth in orthodontic treatments.</p></p>
