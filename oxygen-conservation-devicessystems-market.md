<p><p>The Oxygen Conservation Devices/Systems market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Reservoir Oxygen Delivery Devices**: These devices store supplemental oxygen, releasing it precisely during inhalation, optimizing usage and reducing waste. </p><p>2. **Electromechanical Pulsing Devices**: These advanced systems deliver oxygen in timed pulses aligned with the user's breathing patterns, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>3. **Transtracheal Catheters**: These are small tubes inserted into the trachea, providing direct oxygen delivery to the lungs, promoting effective oxygenation with minimal airflow resistance.</p></p>
