<p><p>The Diabetic Foot Ulcer (DFU) treatment market encompasses three primary ulcer types: neuropathic ulcers, ischemic ulcers, and neuro-ischemic ulcers. Neuropathic ulcers occur due to nerve damage, leading to reduced sensation and unnoticed injuries. Ischemic ulcers result from poor blood flow, causing tissue damage and slow healing. Neuro-ischemic ulcers combine elements of both neuropathy and ischemia, complicating treatment. Effective management for these ulcers includes advanced wound care products, surgeries, and specialized therapies aimed at promoting healing and preventing complications.</p></p>
