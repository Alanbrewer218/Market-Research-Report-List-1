<p><p>The biosolids management market encompasses various treatment methods including aerobic digestion, anaerobic digestion, and nutrient removal or recovery. Aerobic digestion utilizes oxygen to decompose organic matter, producing stabilized biosolids and reduced pathogens. Anaerobic digestion, on the other hand, occurs in the absence of oxygen, generating biogas and organic fertilizers while minimizing odor. Nutrient removal or recovery focuses on extracting valuable nutrients like nitrogen and phosphorus from wastewater, promoting sustainable practices by enabling the reuse of these resources in agriculture and other applications.</p></p>
