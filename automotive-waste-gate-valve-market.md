<p><ul><li>Internal Wastegate</li><li>External Wastegate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wastegate valve market is primarily divided into two types: internal and external wastegates. Internal wastegates are integrated within the turbocharger, regulating boost pressure efficiently and compactly, making them suitable for factory setups. In contrast, external wastegates are mounted separately, allowing for greater control over boost levels and enhanced performance, often favored in high-performance applications. Both types play a crucial role in managing exhaust flow and optimizing engine efficiency, catering to different automotive performance needs.</p></p>
