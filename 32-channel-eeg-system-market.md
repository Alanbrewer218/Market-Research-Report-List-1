<p><p>The 32-channel EEG system market consists of several types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG focuses on recording brain electrical activity using fixed electrodes, ideal for diagnosing epilepsy and other neurological disorders. Video EEG combines brain activity monitoring with video surveillance, enabling correlation of seizures with behavioral events. Dynamic EEG involves continuous monitoring over an extended period, often in a natural environment, providing insights into transient brain activity and improving diagnostic accuracy. These types cater to diverse clinical needs and enhance patient care.</p></p>
