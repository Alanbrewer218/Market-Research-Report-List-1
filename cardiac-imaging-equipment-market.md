<p><p>The cardiac imaging equipment market includes various modalities that enhance heart diagnosis and treatment. X-ray equipment utilizes radiation to capture images for evaluating heart structures. Ultrasound equipment employs sound waves to create real-time images of the heart, aiding in the assessment of cardiac function. CT equipment provides detailed cross-sectional images using X-rays, enabling precise visualization of heart anatomy. MRI equipment uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce high-resolution images, offering insights into heart tissue and blood flow abnormalities.</p></p>
