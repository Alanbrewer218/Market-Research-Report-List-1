<p><p>The Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices market consists of three primary components: glucose meters, test strips, and lancets. Glucose meters are electronic devices used to measure blood sugar levels, providing quick and accurate results. Test strips are disposable components that interact with the blood sample and are crucial for obtaining readings from the meter. Lancets are small, sharp devices used to prick the skin and draw blood. Together, these products facilitate effective diabetes management for individuals.</p></p>
