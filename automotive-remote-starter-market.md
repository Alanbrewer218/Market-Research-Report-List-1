<p><p>The automotive remote starter market can be categorized into three main types: one-way, two-way, and connected car/smartphone systems. One-way starters send a signal from the remote to the vehicle, allowing for basic functions like starting the engine. Two-way systems provide feedback, confirming whether the vehicle has started. Connected car/smartphone systems enhance functionality by allowing users to control the vehicle remotely through apps, providing real-time updates and greater integration with smart technology, ensuring convenience and enhanced security.</p></p>
