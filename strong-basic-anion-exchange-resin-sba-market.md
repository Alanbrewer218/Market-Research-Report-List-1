<p><p>The Strong Basic Anion Exchange Resin (SBA) market comprises two main types: Gel SBA and Macroporous SBA. Gel SBA features a gel-like structure with uniform pore sizes, making it effective for applications requiring high exchange capacity and efficiency in low-concentration solutions. In contrast, Macroporous SBA has a larger pore structure, enabling better flow rates and higher capacity for adsorbing large anions, making it suitable for more diverse and challenging applications, including wastewater treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
