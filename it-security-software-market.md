<p><p>The IT security software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based security software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but often requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific security needs, budget, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
