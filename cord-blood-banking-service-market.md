Private cord blood banking services allow families to store their newborn's stem cells for personal use in the future, providing a sense of security against potential medical needs. In contrast, public cord blood banking services collect and store donated cord blood for public use, making it available for research and treatment of various diseases. Both types play crucial roles in advancing medical research and offering therapeutic options.</p></p>
