<p><p>The scooter tire market features two main types: solid tires and pneumatic tires. Solid tires are made from a single piece of rubber, offering durability and maintenance-free use, making them ideal for urban environments with minimal puncture risk. In contrast, pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing better shock absorption and a smoother ride, making them suitable for varied terrains. Each type caters to different rider preferences and usage conditions, influencing their performance, comfort, and overall user experience.</p></p>
