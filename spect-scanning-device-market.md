<p><p>SPECT scanning devices utilize various radiopharmaceuticals to visualize physiological processes in the body. Iodine-123 is commonly used for thyroid imaging, while Technetium-99m is favored for its versatility in detecting various conditions. Xenon-133 is primarily used for lung ventilation studies. Thallium-201 is employed in myocardial perfusion imaging to assess heart function. Fluorine-18, though more associated with PET scans, can also be relevant in SPECT for certain applications, enhancing diagnosis and treatment planning in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
