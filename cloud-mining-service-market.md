<p><ul><li>Physical Mining Server</li><li>Virtual Private Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud mining services offer two primary market types: physical mining servers and virtual private servers. Physical mining servers involve users renting actual hardware located in data centers, providing them with direct access to mining performance and energy efficiency. In contrast, virtual private servers (VPS) allow users to lease a portion of server resources, enabling mining through shared infrastructure. While VPS offers flexibility and lower costs, physical servers often result in better control and potentially higher returns on investment for serious miners.</p></p>
