<p><p>The EV IGBT modules heatsink market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled heatsinks. Air-cooled heatsinks utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them simpler and less costly, suitable for moderate power applications. In contrast, water-cooled heatsinks employ liquid cooling systems to efficiently manage higher thermal loads, enabling better performance in demanding environments. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in electric vehicles, ensuring reliability and extending the lifespan of IGBT modules.</p></p>
