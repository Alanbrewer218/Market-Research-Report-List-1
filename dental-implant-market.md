<p><p>The dental implant market features two primary types: titanium implants and zirconium implants. Titanium implants, known for their biocompatibility and strength, have been the traditional choice, integrating well with bone and providing durability. Conversely, zirconium implants offer a metal-free alternative, appealing to patients seeking aesthetic solutions due to their tooth-like color and lower risk of corrosion. Both types serve distinct preferences in dental restoration, highlighting advancements in materials that cater to functional and cosmetic dental needs.</p></p>
