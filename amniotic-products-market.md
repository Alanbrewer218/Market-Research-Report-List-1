<p><p>The amniotic products market comprises two primary types: cryopreserved amniotic membranes and dehydrated amniotic membranes. Cryopreserved amniotic membranes are maintained at low temperatures to preserve their cellular structure and growth factors, making them suitable for surgical applications like wound healing and tissue regeneration. Dehydrated amniotic membranes, on the other hand, are processed to remove moisture, extending their shelf life and simplifying storage. Both types provide regenerative properties that support healing and tissue repair in various medical treatments.</p></p>
