<p><strong>The Protein Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edman Degradation</li><li>Mass Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protein sequencing market encompasses various techniques, primarily Edman degradation and mass spectrometry. Edman degradation is a method that sequentially removes amino acids from the N-terminus of a protein, allowing for the identification of the amino acid sequence. In contrast, mass spectrometry offers a more rapid and high-throughput approach, analyzing the mass-to-charge ratios of ionized protein fragments to determine their sequence. Both methods are essential in research and diagnostics, driving advancements in proteomics and biopharmaceutical development.</p></p>
