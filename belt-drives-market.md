<p><p>Belt drives are classified into several market types based on their design and application. </p><p>- ZV (Zero Vibration) belts focus on minimizing vibrations for smoother operation. </p><p>- CV (Constant Velocity) belts ensure consistent speed transmission, ideal for precision applications. </p><p>- CR (Constant Radius) belts maintain a fixed radius for reliable performance. </p><p>- CL (Constant Load) belts are designed to handle specific loads efficiently. </p><p>These types cater to diverse industrial needs, improving performance and efficiency in various machinery.</p></p>
