<p><p>The elastic bonding sealant market comprises three main types: rubber-based, resin-based, and oil-based sealants. Rubber-type sealants offer flexibility and durability, making them ideal for applications requiring high movement tolerance. Resin-type sealants provide superior adhesion and are often used in construction and automotive industries for their strength and stability. Oil-based sealants are known for their water-resistant properties and ease of application, frequently used in maintenance and repair tasks. Each type serves specific needs based on application requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
