<p><p>Neutral Density (ND) filters are categorized based on their light reduction capabilities. ND filters less than 0.3 allow minimal light blockage, suitable for protecting lens and achieving slight depth of field effects. ND filters from 0.3 to 3.0 provide moderate light attenuation, ideal for balancing exposure in bright conditions while maintaining motion blur. ND filters exceeding 3.0 significantly reduce light, enabling long exposure photography during daylight, allowing creative effects like silky smooth water or blurred movement in subjects.</p></p>
