<p><ul><li>E-Beam Mask Exposure</li><li>Laser Beam Mask Exposure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Exposure Equipment market consists of two primary types: E-Beam Mask Exposure and Laser Beam Mask Exposure. E-Beam Mask Exposure utilizes focused electron beams to create high-resolution patterns on masks, enabling the production of intricate and precise designs, essential for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, Laser Beam Mask Exposure employs laser light to expose photoresist-coated masks, offering flexibility and efficiency in production. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for intricate chip designs in various electronic applications.</p></p>
