<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gear Hydraulic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>Gear Ring Pump</li><li>Screw Spindle Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gear hydraulic pump market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. External gear pumps feature two intermeshing gears to generate flow, ideal for high-viscosity fluids. Internal gear pumps use an inner gear rotating within an outer gear, offering a more compact design suitable for low-pressure applications. Gear ring pumps employ a ring of gears to create movement, while screw spindle pumps utilize helical screws for efficient fluid transfer. Each type is valued for its reliability, efficiency, and diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
