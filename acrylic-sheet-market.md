<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheet market is primarily categorized into two types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are created by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in a thicker, more durable material with superior optical clarity and strength, ideal for applications like signage and displays. In contrast, extruded acrylic sheets are produced by forcing acrylic through a die, making them less expensive and suitable for applications requiring lightweight, flexible materials, such as glazing and general fabrication.</p></p>
