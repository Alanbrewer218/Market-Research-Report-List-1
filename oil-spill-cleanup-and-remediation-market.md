<p><p>The oil spill cleanup and remediation market consists of several key types. Physical methods include containment and recovery techniques, like booms and skimmers, to minimize spread and remove oil. Chemical methods involve dispersants that break down oil into smaller droplets, enhancing natural degradation. Biological approaches utilize microorganisms or bioremediation agents to metabolize oil, promoting natural recovery. Mechanical methods encompass equipment and tools designed for direct removal and cleaning, ensuring effective response and restoration of affected environments.</p></p>
