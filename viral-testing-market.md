<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Viral Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) Test</li><li>Immunochromatographic Assay</li><li>RT-PCR-Based Test</li><li>Agglutination Assay</li><li>Flow-Through Assay</li><li>Solid-Phase Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viral testing market encompasses several methodologies. Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) tests utilize fluorescent dyes to detect viral antigens. Immunochromatographic assays employ a simple, rapid format for antigen detection through capillary action. RT-PCR-based tests amplify viral RNA for precise identification. Agglutination assays utilize the clumping of particles to indicate viral presence. Flow-through assays allow liquid samples to pass through a membrane for detection, while solid-phase assays immobilize antigens for analysis, offering a range of sensitivity and specificity in viral diagnostics.</p></p>
