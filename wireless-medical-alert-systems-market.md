<p><p>Wireless medical alert systems utilize various communication technologies to ensure timely assistance for users. Zigbee networks provide low-power, short-range connectivity, ideal for home environments. Wi-Fi offers broader coverage and high-speed data transmission, allowing users to connect through their existing home networks. Bluetooth is favored for its energy efficiency in personal devices, facilitating close-range communication. Cellular technology enables devices to work independently of local networks, ensuring reliable connectivity even outside. Other emerging technologies may include RFID and satellite systems, enhancing versatility.</p></p>
