<p><p>The sensor-based glucose measuring system market encompasses two main types: self-monitoring glucose measuring systems and continuous glucose measuring systems. Self-monitoring systems allow individuals to check their blood glucose levels at specific times using portable devices, providing immediate results. In contrast, continuous glucose measuring systems offer real-time monitoring by using sensors that track glucose levels throughout the day and night, transmitting data to mobile devices. Both types enhance diabetes management through improved accuracy and convenience for users.</p></p>
