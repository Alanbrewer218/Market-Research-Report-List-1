<p><p>The PVC roofing membrane market consists of two primary types: glass fiber reinforced PVC membranes and non-reinforced PVC membranes. Glass fiber reinforced PVC membranes offer enhanced durability, strength, and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, non-reinforced PVC membranes are lighter and more flexible, providing ease of installation and cost-effectiveness for less rigorous environments. Both types are widely used in commercial and residential roofing systems due to their weather resistance and long lifespan.</p></p>
