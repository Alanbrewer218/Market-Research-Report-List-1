<p><p>The humic-based biostimulants market includes three main types: humic acid, fulvic acid, and potassium humate. Humic acid enhances soil structure and nutrient retention, promoting plant growth and health. Fulvic acid is a smaller molecule that helps in nutrient absorption and improves microbial activity. Potassium humate combines the benefits of potassium with humic substances, enhancing soil fertility and plant stress resistance. Together, these biostimulants improve soil quality, crop yield, and environmental sustainability in agricultural practices.</p></p>
