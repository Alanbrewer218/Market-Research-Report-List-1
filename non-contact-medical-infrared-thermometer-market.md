<p><p>The non-contact medical infrared thermometer market comprises primarily two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure temperature by scanning the forehead surface, providing quick and convenient readings without skin contact, making them ideal for mass screenings. Ear type thermometers, on the other hand, utilize infrared technology to gauge temperature from the ear canal, offering accurate readings for individuals. Both types are widely used in clinical and home settings, emphasizing ease of use and efficiency in monitoring health.</p></p>
