<p><p>Sperm separation systems are crucial for enhancing reproductive technologies. On-chip systems utilize microfluidic technology for efficient, high-throughput separation on a compact platform. Electrophoresis employs electric fields to separate sperm based on charge and size, allowing for precise selection. Dielectrophoresis leverages non-uniform electric fields to manipulate sperm based on their dielectric properties, ensuring high purity and viability. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in assisted reproduction, improving outcomes for fertility treatments and research.</p></p>
