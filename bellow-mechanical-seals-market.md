<p><p>The Bellow Mechanical Seals market is primarily segmented into two types: Pusher Type Seals and Non-Pusher Seals. Pusher Type Seals feature a dynamic element that maintains constant contact with the rotating shaft, compensating for wear and providing enhanced sealing performance. In contrast, Non-Pusher Seals lack this dynamic element, relying on minimal contact and often exhibiting simpler designs. Both types serve to prevent leakage in various industrial applications, with choices driven by factors like operating conditions, maintenance needs, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
