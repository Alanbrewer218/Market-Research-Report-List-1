<p><p>The IoT LVDT sensors market encompasses two primary types: DC LVDT sensors and AC LVDT sensors. DC LVDT sensors use direct current to provide precise measurements and are known for their reliability in low-power applications. In contrast, AC LVDT sensors operate with alternating current, offering advantages in high-speed measurements and greater accuracy over longer distances. Both sensor types leverage IoT integration, enabling real-time data transmission and enhanced analysis, making them vital in various industrial applications such as automation and robotics.</p></p>
