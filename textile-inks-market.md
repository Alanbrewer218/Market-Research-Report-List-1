<p><strong>The Textile Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile inks market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Reactive dye inks are ideal for cotton and natural fibers, chemically bonding with substrates for vibrant colors. Acidic inks are primarily used on protein fibers like wool and silk, offering brilliant shades. Paint inks provide opacity and durability, suitable for various surfaces. Dispersion inks are used for synthetic fabrics, ensuring colorfastness, while sublimation inks transition from solid to gas, best for polyester and coated materials, delivering vivid prints.</p></p>
