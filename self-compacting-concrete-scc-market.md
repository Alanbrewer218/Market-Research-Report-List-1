<p><strong>The Self-compacting Concrete (SCC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Type Self-Compacting Concrete</li><li>Viscosity Agent Type Self-Compacting Concrete</li><li>Combination Type Self-Compacting Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC) is a highly fluid mixture that can flow and consolidate under its weight, requiring no vibration for compaction. The market for SCC consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Powder Type SCC** utilizes fine powders and aggregates for enhanced flowability. </p><p>2. **Viscosity Agent Type SCC** incorporates additives that enhance viscosity, ensuring stability and preventing segregation. </p><p>3. **Combination Type SCC** blends both powder and viscosity agents, offering versatility for various construction applications, ensuring durability and structural integrity.</p></p>
