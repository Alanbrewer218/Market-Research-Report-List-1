<p><p>The Steering Roll Connector (SRC) market comprises three main types: Spiral Wound Type, U-Turn Roller Type, and U-Turn Dummy Type. The Spiral Wound Type features a coiled design that allows for flexible movement and stress distribution in steering systems. The U-Turn Roller Type incorporates rollers for smoother directional changes, enhancing maneuverability. The U-Turn Dummy Type serves as a placeholder or support in steering assemblies, ensuring structural integrity. Together, these types cater to diverse automotive steering requirements, improving performance and reliability.</p></p>
