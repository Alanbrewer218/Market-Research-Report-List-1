Living cell-based printing, on the other hand, utilizes actual cells to craft organs, closely mimicking natural tissues and enhancing functionality. Both types aim to revolutionize transplantation and regenerative medicine, addressing organ shortages and advancing personalized therapies for various medical conditions.</p></p>
