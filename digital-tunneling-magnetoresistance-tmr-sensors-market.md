<p><p>Digital tunneling magnetoresistance (TMR) sensors are classified into three main types based on their operational characteristics: omnipolar, bipolar, and unipolar. Omnipolar sensors detect magnetic fields in both polarities, providing versatile applications. Bipolar sensors can distinguish between two distinct magnetic states, enabling precise position sensing. Unipolar sensors respond only to one polarity of the magnetic field, ideal for specific applications like end-of-stroke detection. Each type caters to unique needs across industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
