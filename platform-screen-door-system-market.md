<p><p>The Platform Screen Door (PSD) System market is categorized into two main types: Full Height and Half Height. Full Height PSDs extend from the floor to the ceiling, enhancing safety by preventing accidents and improving climate control in underground stations. In contrast, Half Height PSDs are shorter, allowing for open-air ventilation while still providing some safety against falls or accidents. Both types are crucial for modern transit systems, improving passenger experience and operational efficiency while promoting safety.</p></p>
