<p><strong>The Soda Feldspar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plagioclase Feldspar</li><li>K-feldspar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soda feldspar market is primarily divided into two types: plagioclase feldspar and K-feldspar. Plagioclase feldspar contains varying proportions of sodium and calcium, making it valuable in the ceramics and glass industries for its low thermal expansion properties. K-feldspar, rich in potassium, is favored for its high melting point and suitability in ceramics, porcelain, and glass production. Both types are essential for enhancing the physical properties of end products, impacting their performance in various applications.</p></p>
