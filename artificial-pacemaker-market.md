<p><p>The artificial pacemaker market encompasses several types of devices designed to regulate heart rhythms. Temporary cardiac pacing is utilized for short-term needs, often in hospital settings. Single chamber cardiac pacing targets one heart chamber, typically the right atrium or ventricle, providing basic rhythm support. Double-chamber cardiac pacing coordinates both atria and ventricles, ensuring more synchronized heart function. Other pacemaker types include biventricular and leadless systems, which offer advanced functionalities for complex cardiac conditions, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
