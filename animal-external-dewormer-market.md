<p><p>The animal external dewormer market is categorized primarily into two types: injections and unguents. Injections are administered directly into the animal's body, offering rapid absorption and effective parasite control. They are often preferred for severe infestations or when immediate results are required. Unguents, on the other hand, are topical applications designed to be absorbed through the skin, providing a convenient method for routine deworming. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining animal health and preventing the spread of parasites in livestock.</p></p>
