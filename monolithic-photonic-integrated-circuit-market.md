<p><strong>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) market is segmented into two types: Passive and Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. Passive PICs do not require external power to operate; they include components like waveguides, filters, and couplers, primarily used for signal routing and processing. In contrast, Active PICs incorporate electronically controlled elements such as lasers, modulators, and detectors, enabling dynamic functions like signal generation and manipulation. Both types play crucial roles in telecommunications, sensing, and data processing applications, driving innovation in photonic technologies.</p></p>
